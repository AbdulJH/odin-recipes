Baked Potato

Description

"The best baked potato recipe - perfectly crispy and flavorful on the outside, soft and fluffy on the inside, and so flavorful and delicious."

Ingredients

  • Russet potatoes (can other type of potato)
  • Butter or olive oil
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly-ground black pepper

  2. Prepare your baking sheet.
  3. Poke your potato all over with a fork.
  4. First bake
  5. Brush the potato with melted butter (or oil).
  6. Second bake.
  7. Cut, squeeze, fluff, season and serve!
